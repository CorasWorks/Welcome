Welcome to the CorasWorks GitHub Environment!

With the the release of the CorasWorks Application Service (CAPS), our customers now have a flexible, feature-rich API that can be used for creating anything from the simplest SharePoint widgets up to full-blown Enterprise applications.

Coupled with the benefits of GitHub, we can not only share our examples and the generic files we often start from when building solutions, but invite the myriad of bright, talented users within our customer organizations to provide direct suggestions for improvements or even fork & update something themselves!

What's Here?

Based on the CorasWorks products your organization has licenses, you'll be able to access XML/XSLT, JS, CSS & HTML for a wide variety of uses.

Completed our "Designing and Building Applications with CAPS" training course? You'll get access to even more, including a downloadable copy of the Report Designer template and all the source files.

Own one of our Solutions like Project Portfolio Management (PPM), CorasWorks Idea Management (CIM), Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity management (IDIQ) or Capture and Proposal Management? Solution-specific repos are also available to make it easy for you to collaborate with us and other users of the same Solution.
